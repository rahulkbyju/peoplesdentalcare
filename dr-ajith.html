About Dr. Ajith

Dr. Ajith K.S is a highly accomplished dental professional with over 15 years of specialized experience in cosmetic and restorative dentistry. After completing his Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) and Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) in Prosthodontics, he pursued advanced training through a Fellowship in Cosmetic Dentistry to refine his expertise in smile design and aesthetic procedures. His passion for creating beautiful, natural-looking smiles has made him a trusted name in the field, with thousands of satisfied patients who have experienced life-changing transformations under his care.

At People's Dental Care, Dr. Ajith combines artistry with precision, utilizing the latest technology and techniques in cosmetic dentistry, including porcelain veneers, smile makeovers, dental implants, and full mouth rehabilitation. His patient-centered approach emphasizes comfort, transparency, and personalized treatment plans tailored to each individual's unique needs and goals.
